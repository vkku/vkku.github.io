# Munsyari
### The views, the peaks, the rhododendron speaks

#### Itinerary (05 Days - 04 Nights, 25 March - 29 March)
1. Moradabad to Kathgodam via 12040 Kathgodam Shatabdi
2. Kathgodam to Berinag, Taxi driven by Sandeep
3. Berinag to Munsyari via Thal, Taxi
4. Day 01 at Munsyari, Acclimatization at homestay
5. Day 02 at Munsyari, Trek to Lal Singh Gair, Verdant peak views, rhododendron blossoms
6. Day 03 at Munsyari, Visit to :
   1. Masterji Museum
   2. Handicrafts village Darkot, bought woollen muffler and cap
   3. Bouna village farmers association, bought himalayan herbs
   4. Nanda Devi Temple, in unison with Panchachuli
7. Return to Moradabad via taxi to Jauljibi(Scenic views, road along gori ganga), Pithoragarh and bus from Haldwani
